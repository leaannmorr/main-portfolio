About Me.

I have over 15 years in the technology industry and developed a deep passion for bridging the gap between business users and builders of technology solutions. I've found that most technology initiatives fail due to poor processes and mismatched expectations about needs and deadlines.

Why waste money?! Sometimes the best answer is a good plan, a simple solution, tweaking of processes, and/or stepping back to ask the right questions. I help small businesses, start-ups and non-profits maximize resources when identifying, evaluating and implementing new processes and technology solutions.

Let me help you ask the right questions and guide you to the right outcomes for success.
